About Us

At MYMETO, we strive to redefine the way you experience and understand weather updates. What started as a unique project for personal development has evolved into a vision for the future—delivering personalized, AI-driven weather insights that go beyond the ordinary.

Our Mission: To provide users with an intuitive, user-friendly platform that combines accurate weather data with personalized recommendations. We aim to enhance daily decision-making by integrating weather forecasts with lifestyle suggestions.

Our Vision: We are working on transforming MYMETO into a highly interactive AI-powered platform that not only informs but assists users in planning their day more efficiently. Imagine receiving recommendations for what to wear, when to travel, or even alerts tailored to your schedule—all powered by advanced AI.

Our Commitment: We take privacy seriously, storing only essential information such as your name, email, and favorite cities. As we grow, we aim to maintain transparency and provide value-driven services to our users.

Join us on this journey to make weather updates not just informative but truly impactful.
